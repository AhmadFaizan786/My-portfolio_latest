About me

I'm a final year Computer Science Engineering student with a strong focus on front-end web development. My fascination lies in crafting user interfaces that are not only visually appealing but also intuitive and user-friendly. I'm fascinated by the world of web development, where creativity and code come together to shape the online experiences we have.
Beyond academics, I'm actively involved in my college's developer community. As the ex-graphics lead at the Google

Developers Student Club and technical coordinator at the GeeksforGeeks student chapter, I thrive in collaborative environments, bridging the gap between engineering and design. There's immense satisfaction in turning ideas into beautiful, functional web products that come alive online.

When I'm not coding, you might find me keeping up with current trends over a steaming cup of chai to stay creative , or crossing off another item on my bucket list.
